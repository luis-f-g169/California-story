California storytelling project · Opportunities

A never-ending trail of opportunities in California.

My parents immigrated from Mexico to California in search of better opportunities. This site turns that idea into a visual trail: one main path (California) with side paths branching into key opportunities that shaped who I am.

Scroll down to follow the path. Each stop unfolds in chronological order—from early chances my family took, to my current path as a Computer Science major, and the futures I’m still walking toward.
